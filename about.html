Our Story

Founded with a simple mission: to cut through the confusion of internet and cable shopping. We realized that consumers were overwhelmed by complex packages, hidden fees, and aggressive sales tactics from major providers.

ISP Consultant was born from the frustration of seeing families and businesses overpay for services that didn't meet their needs. Our founders, with over 15 years of combined experience in telecommunications, decided to create an independent consultancy that puts customers first.
